You are implementing a FULL Overtime Management Module in ZenJO HRMS.

This is a critical operational and financial module.
It must integrate tightly with shifts, attendance, and payroll.

Do NOT build a simple overtime form.
Build a complete overtime tracking, approval, and calculation system.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PRIMARY GOAL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Create an Overtime system where HR can:

* monitor overtime activity
* track overtime records (automatic and manual)
* enforce overtime policies
* manage approvals
* calculate overtime payments
* integrate with payroll
* generate reports

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3) OVERTIME RECORDING METHODS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Support two main methods:

A) AUTOMATIC (FROM ATTENDANCE)

* system calculates overtime after shift end
* based on shift rules
* respects break time
* excludes invalid records

B) MANUAL / REQUEST-BASED

Employee submits request:

* Date
* Start time
* End time
* Reason
* optional attachment

Store request separately before approval.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4) APPROVAL WORKFLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Standard flow:

Employee → Manager → HR

Each step can:

* approve
* reject
* request changes

System must:

* track approval history
* track approvers and timestamps
* prevent unapproved OT from being paid

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5) OVERTIME RULES ENGINE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Create configurable rules:

* OT starts after X working hours (example: after 8 hours/day)
* maximum overtime hours per day/week
* overtime rate rules:

  * 125% for first hours
  * 150% after threshold
* optional different rates for:

  * weekends
  * holidays

Rules must:

* be configurable per company
* optionally be linked to shift type

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6) SHIFT & ATTENDANCE INTEGRATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

System must:

* read shift start and end time
* detect actual worked hours from attendance
* calculate overtime automatically when:
  worked hours > shift hours

Important:

* use shift rules as source of truth
* do NOT duplicate logic
