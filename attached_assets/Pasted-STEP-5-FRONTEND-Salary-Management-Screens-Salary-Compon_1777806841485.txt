═══════════════════════════════════
CONSTRAINTS
═══════════════════════════════════
- All monetary amounts: DECIMAL(12,3) in DB, displayed with 3 decimal places.
- Always western digits for numbers — never Arabic-Indic numerals (١٢٣ → 123).
- Approved payroll runs are IMMUTABLE — no editing payslips after approval.
- Salary data visible ONLY to hradmin + payrolladmin — server-enforced masking.
- Formula evaluation: NEVER use eval() or any dynamic code execution.
- SSC and tax rates MUST come from system_configurations — never hardcode.
- Overtime amounts in payroll: only pull APPROVED requests — pending ones are excluded.
